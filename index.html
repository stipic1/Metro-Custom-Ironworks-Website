About Us

The history behind our operation.

In 2002, Malden Stipic decided to take his talent working with wrought iron specialties a step further, and formed Metro Custom Iron Works (MCIW). In addition to his already 25 years’ experience, MCIW has continued the tradition in bringing specialized iron fabrication to residential and commercial customers throughout the Northwest.

Ornamental ironwork has been around for centuries and is still very popular today, primarily because of its traditional and elegant look, but also by its superior durability and strength. MCIW is a versatile and full service steel shop handling jobs both large and small, and utilizing top quality materials with high-end craftsmanship. Whether it be simple or grand, MCIW can design, manufacture, and install your custom iron vision. Please inquire today.

Frquently Asked Questions

Answers to some common questions.

1. Do you install your own work?
A: Yes, we do install our own work, and we won’t leave your job site until the work is installed exactly the way you want it. We will also do the fabrication only for customers that would like to do the installation themselves.

2. What kinds of finishes are available?
A: We can provide any finish that can be applied to steel. Typically, customers will choose powder coating or galvanizing because of their high durability.

3. What are the advantages of steel products (railing, fence, gates, etc.) as opposed to wood?
A: While wood may be the cheaper option at first glance, steel is much more durable and will save you money in the end. With the rainy weather that we often have in the Pacific Northwest, wood will weaken over time and will require frequent coating to protect it. Steel, on the other hand, will stand the test of time and requires much less maintenance. Not to mention the elegance and value that a steel railing, fence, or gate will add to your property!

4. What forms of payment do you accept?
A: We accept cash and checks only at this time.
